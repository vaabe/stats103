These notes are supplements to STAT-UB 103, an introductory course in probability, statistics, and regression modeling. We use R to teach the course. The first three notes give a pithy introduction to R programming. Feedback is most welcome—please get in touch or submit a pull request.

Made in collaboration with Joshua Loftus. Here is the syllabus. Theme inspired by Tufte Handouts.
